The Bride's Side Family of the bride

Shah Family

Sonal Shah (Mother)

The lady with the most charming smile and who believes in "Talk Less and Do more". She is her daughter's first and the bestest of all friends. She is gifted with this unique sense of telepathically knowing exactly what her family wishes for. Even before we say it, she has it ready in front of us. She is the heart of the family, silently beating and smoothly functioning the family.

Nidhi Shah (The Bride)

Creativity defines her. That's what she is the most passionate about (Of course, apart from Adi). She does something which actually has an impact on the world, treating people. She is known for either doing something very passionately or not doing it at all. She is everyone's favorite. She has a hidden talent, Management. She does that almost effortlessly. That's Nidhi for you, the fevicol of our long distance relationship :)
Quick fact: She proposed Aditya at Mumbai Airport, in front of a crowd. Takes a lot of effort to do that.

Pankaj Shah (Father)

A self made man, 50 plus but still a child at heart. Always keeps a joyful atmosphere around him. To his daughter's delight, he is into the business of making "Diamond Jewellery";):)). He has raised his two daughters like two princesses and fulfilled every wish of theirs. His "Never Say a No" attitude wins everyone's heart.

Ruchi Shah Gadhia (Sister)

A Jewelry designer by profession, she thrives to keep her loved ones happy and loves to surprise them with gifts. She is fond of Coffee, Reading, Dancing and Travelling. Oh!!! And how can one forget.... "Food"!!

Pratik Gadhia (Brother-in-law)

The latest addition to Shah Family. Jovial by nature, Adventurous, loves to Travel & Trek, Talkative. He can give a tough competition to Nidhi when it comes to talking !

Jay Modi (Brother)

Budding enterpreneur, quora'ist, economist. He is the in-house comedian and singer. His song collection will blow your mind.
